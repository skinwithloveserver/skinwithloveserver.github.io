Policies

Arrival

Please arrive on-time to your appointment to allow time to complete any forms needed and to settle in. New clients please arrive 15 minutes before your appointment as you will be required to complete a variety of forms, this will ensure safe and optimal treatments. All forms and records are strictly confidential and kept in your private client folder for future visits.

Reschedule/Cancel/No Show Appointment

Your appointments and time are very important to me and are reserved especially for you. I completely understand that there are unexpected things that can come up in life. If you need to reschedule or cancel an appointment, I simply ask that you do so at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment. I ask for a deposit upon booking to secure your spot within the schedule. I am by appointment only and if you do not cancel or re-schedule at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment you will lose your deposit.

Please understand that when you fail to reschedule or cancel your appointment without giving me enough notice that I miss the opportunity to schedule that time towards another client. Please understand it is your responsibility to remember your appointment dates and times.

Sanitization and Sterilization

Sanitization is a top priority at Skin With Love Studio. I use professional medical grade disinfectants, sanitizers, and sterilants. Tools are never reused before they are thoroughly cleaned.

Method of Payment

I accept Cash, Visa, MasterCard, AMEX, Apple Pay and Interac. Gratuities are not included in the service price.

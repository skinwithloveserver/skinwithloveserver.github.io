Policies

Arrival

Please arrive on-time to your appointment to allow time to complete any forms needed and to settle in. New clients please arrive 5 minutes before your appointment as you will be required to complete a variety of forms, this will ensure safe and optimal treatments. All forms and records are strictly confidential and kept in your private client folder for future visits.

Arriving Late

Arriving to your treatment late may result in a shorter treatment time. This is necessary in order for me to stay on schedule and accommodate my next scheduled appointment. Shortened appointments will not result in a lowered price for your treatment.

If you are 15 minutes late to your appointment then you will lose your deposit and the appointment will be cancelled and rescheduled with a new deposit. Please be aware that this is considered a cancellation/no show.

We want to provide the best service, and 15-20 mins after scheduled time will not allow us enough time to complete your service properly. Please be aware.

Reschedule/Cancel/No Show Appointment

Your appointments and time are very important to me and are reserved especially for you. I completely understand that there are unexpected things that can come up in life. If you need to reschedule or cancel an appointment, I simply ask that you do so at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment. I ask for a deposit upon booking to secure your spot within the schedule. I am by appointment only and if you do not cancel or re-schedule at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment you will lose your deposit. If you do give us a 24hr notice to cancel we will reschedule your appointment for another date that will work for you and transfer over your existing deposit. Please note that all deposits are non-refundable.

Please understand that when you fail to reschedule or cancel your appointment without giving me enough notice that I miss the opportunity to schedule that time towards another client. Please understand it is your responsibility to remember your appointment dates and times.

Sanitization and Sterilization

Sanitization is a top priority at Skin With Love Studio. I use professional medical grade disinfectants, sanitizers, and sterilants. Tools are never reused before they are thoroughly cleaned.

No linens are ever reused before they are thoroughly cleaned. If at any time an item cannot be thoroughly cleaned after use it is disposed of.

Sick

I consistently provide an environment for myself and for my clients that is health aware, safe, clean, and disease free. I ask clients to please cancel and reschedule their appointment if they are not well and have any kind of contagious viral or bacterial infection. Coming into Skin With Love Studio when you are unwell puts myself and other clients at risk of exposure to also getting infected.

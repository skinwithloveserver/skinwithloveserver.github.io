Our Facial and Skin Treatments

Consistent facial treatments always leads you to the best skin possible and amazing results. Skin takes commitment & Skin With Love Studio is committed to providing results-oriented treatments to meet your skin's needs & concerns.

Hydrafacial MD- $160 (1hr)

HydraFacial deep cleanses, exfoliates and extracts blackheads, whiteheads, and other environmental impurities, while simultaneously rehydrating and moisturizing the skin. This process utilizes skin enhancing serums (antioxidants, peptides and Hyaluronic Acid) for a total skin rejuvenation. The unique Vortex-Fusion serum delivery system is what sets the HydraFacialMD apart from other procedures.

Glow & Go - $99 (30 mins)

Deeply cleanse and exfoliate your skin with a facial that is customized with nourishing products and ingredients chosen specifically for your skin type. This is a customizable treatment that includes extractions, masks, serums and more.

Glow Getter - $199 (1hr)

Have you ever wanted to enhance your natural beauty? Well this is the treatment for you! This is a customizable treatment that is designed just for you, using only the best methods for you to reach your highest potential with your skin - giving you hydrated and radiant skin by the end!

Chemical Peel - $145 (1hr)

Improve the look and feel of your skin and shed dead skin cells off to give your skin a youthful glow. This will dramatically improve the skin and brighten uneven skin tone, diminish fine lines and wrinkles, reduce blemishes and acne scarring, and target problems that can make us look older.

Angel Skin Signature - $165 (1hr)

Elevate your skin to reach its softest, hydrated, lifted, and lightest potential - just like an angel's touch would be. Including LED therapy, relaxing massage, extractions, masks and so much more. You'll be growing wings by the end of it!

Dermaplaning Facial - $115 (1hr)

Dermaplaning is a method of physical exfoliation. A sterile, surgical scalpel is used to gently "shave" the skin's surface, removing the top-most layer of dead skin along with fine, vellus hair (a.k.a peach fuzz). It also helps to remove any dead skin, leaving your skin super soft and allowing for your products to penetrate deeper. This facial comes with, Cryo Therapy, LED Therapy, massage, extractions and so much more leaving you with your natural glow!

Teeth Whitening - $215 (1HR)

Looking for a brighter smile? This is the treatment for you! This is a customizable application and done with blue LED! Results last 3-6 months depending on your oral health. You get anywhere between 2-10 shades brighter depending on your teeth and enamel.
